# Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard
---
### Project Overview
---
#### The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze operational efficiency and patient trends within an emergency department setting.
#### This analysis covers 9,216 patient records over a 19-month period (April 2023 – October 2024). The dashboard transforms raw healthcare data into meaningful insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Project Objective

#### To analyze emergency room operations and patient trends using Power BI, providing actionable insights to improve efficiency, reduce wait times, and enhance patient satisfaction.
## 📈 Key Insights:
#### Wait Time: Average of 5.3 minutes, yet satisfaction remains moderate at 4.99/10.
#### Referrals: Driven primarily by General Practice and Orthopedics.
#### Demographics: Most visits from the 30-39 age group across diverse backgrounds.
#### Admissions: High acuity with nearly 50% of patients requiring hospitalization.

# 🔹 Impact
#### Resource Management: Prep beds for the 50% admission conversion rate.
#### Patient Experience: Focus on care quality to bridge the gap between fast wait times and low satisfaction scores
